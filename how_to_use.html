How to Use

The FULL information on how to use Championship Manager will be available soon. Please check back later for instructions and guidelines on using this platform effectively.

What is this app

This app is made for managing online racing championships. Feel free to view latest leaderboards, championships or race results.

Registration

To register, you need to click on Login in the header or footer and under the form is a link to registration form. There you need to fill out your information, such as email and password. After successful registration, you must log in with the same information as you registered.

Setting up profile

After logging in you can move to page Edit my profile, where you can edit your basic information. If you are a driver, you can click on the button in the bottom of the page, but be aware, that then your personal information will become visible. You can change name, nationality, etc.

Changing email address

If you want to change the email you have associated with your profile, you can do so. But, you need to confirm those changes in incoming mail on both emails. After confirmations, log in with the new information.

Changing profile picture

After choosing the picture and clicking on upload, you will not see the picture straight away. The change may take some minutes, depending on the server database traffic. If you want to change the current picture with a newer, just upload it and it will change automatically.
